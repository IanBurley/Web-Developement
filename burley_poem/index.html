Man of Steel

In a world where shadows creep and fears take flight, Stands a figure of strength, a beacon of light. A hero unyielding, both brave and true, The Man of Steel, with skies of blue.

With a heart of gold and fists of might, He soars through the heavens, a symbol of right. From the city's depths to the highest peak, He defends the innocent, the weak.

Born of Krypton's distant star, He bears the weight of a world's scars. A legacy forged in the crucible of fate, He rises above, love and justice his trait.

His cape billows in the winds that gust, A guardian's touch, in him we trust. A fortress of solitude, his hidden retreat, Contemplating a destiny so bittersweet.

Yet battles rage, foes emerge anew, Challenges that only the brave can undo. With unwavering valor, he stands the test, In the S-shielded emblem, we find rest.

Through the darkest nights and days so bright, The Man of Steel fights the endless fight. A symbol of hope, a beacon of might, Guiding us through the darkness, into the light.
